Clarity Psychological Services provides assessment and treatment services out of the Lower Mainland, BC. We are currently providing services via secure telehealth platform at this time.

We are dedicated to providing dignity-affirming, culturally sensitive care in the service of your personal goals and wellbeing. We welcome individuals from all backgrounds, spiritual denominations, and relationship and sexual orientations (i.e., LGBTQ2SIA+ and multi-amory friendly).

Clarity Clinicians

Dr. Jonathan Blasberg , R. Psych (2350)

Dr. Blasberg completed his Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from the University of British Columbia and his pre-doctoral internship was completed at The Ottawa Hospital Rehabilitation Centre and the University of Ottawa Heart Institute, treating anxiety and depression in patients coping with serious illness and/or injury. Dr. Blasberg uses a combination of cognitive-behavioural, interpersonal and mindfulness-based approaches to psychological treatment, and takes pride in developing effective working relationships with clients to help them improve their quality of life and meet their personal wellness goals. Contact Dr. Blasberg today.

Dr. Jill Nesbitt, R. Psych (2421)

Dr. Nesbitt’s work experience encompasses a variety of settings, including community mental health-dialectical behaviour therapy and intensive outpatient teams, university counseling center, university-based hospital, long-term care facilities, and rehabilitative health center. She fosters an integrative and collaborative environment in which she works with clients to identify the underlying causes of their challenges. By using evidence-based techniques, Dr. Nesbitt empowers her clients to overcome their difficulties and move forward in their lives. Creating a supportive and validating space is a cornerstone of Dr. Nesbitt’s practice and she strives to provide a nurturing environment that promotes growth and healing for her clients. Together, Dr. Nesbitt helps her clients explore their experiences, uncover their strengths, and develop strategies to help them overcome and thrive beyond their current difficulties. Dr. Nesbitt provides video- and telephone psychotherapy appointments and currently has limited evening and daytime openings available. Please feel free to contact Dr. Nesbitt at (778) 874-3964 or drjillnesbitt@gmail.com to schedule an initial appointment or to obtain more information.

Dr. Jenny Tang, R. Psych (1738)

Dr. Jenny Tang is a Registered Psychologist with almost 20 years of clinical experience; she has worked with diverse adults in different settings and my primary focus over the years has been to provide comprehensive assessment services to help support clients in addressing their mental health needs. My approach is diligent, gentle, and persistent; I hope that we can work together to communicate your perspectives in a way that is helpful for both yourself and your service providers. I am presently providing the following assessment services: diagnosis, treatment, personality and cognitive functioning, vocational rehabilitation, pre-sentence psychological reports, and incapability assessments (under the Guardianship Act, Part 3). Dr. Tang is not currently accepting new referrals at this time. She can be contacted at: drjennytang@gmail.com
