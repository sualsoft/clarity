Clarity Psychological Services provides assessment and treatment services out of the Lower Mainland, BC. We are currently providing services via secure telehealth platform and in person at our Burnaby, BC location.

We are dedicated to providing dignity-affirming, culturally sensitive care in the service of your personal goals and wellbeing. We welcome individuals from all backgrounds, spiritual denominations, and relationship and sexual orientations (i.e., LGBTQ2SIA+ and multi-amory friendly). We are offering remote treatment and assessment services to individuals in British Columbia and Nova Scotia at this time.

Contact us today to see how we can be of service to you.

Clarity Clinicians

Dr. Jonathan Blasberg , R. Psych (2350)

Dr. Jonathan Blasberg is the founder and clinical director of Clarity Psychological Services. He is a registered psychologist with the College of Health and Care Professionals of British Columbia

Dr. Blasberg completed his Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from the University of British Columbia and his pre-doctoral internship was completed at The Ottawa Hospital Rehabilitation Centre and the University of Ottawa Heart Institute, treating anxiety and depression in patients coping with serious illness and/or injury. In providing assessment services, Dr. Blasberg is dedicated to understanding your unique experiences and circumstances and communicating your personal story clearly in a way that you, other health professionals, and other stakeholders can understand. In providing treatment, Dr. Blasberg uses a combination of cognitive-behavioural, interpersonal, and mindfulness-based approaches to psychological treatment, and takes pride in developing effective working relationships with clients to help them improve their quality of life and meet their personal wellness goals. Contact Dr. Blasberg today.
