Clarity Psychological Services offers individually tailored treatment services to meet your needs.

This typically involves meeting regularly with a mental health professional to work on improving your overall wellbeing and happiness, alleviate anxiety, reduce emotional distress, recover from stressful events, and improve resiliency.

All therapeutic approaches provided by Clarity psychologists are scientifically grounded and based on a blend of multiple theoretical models of behaviour, including cognitive-behavioural, interpersonal, humanistic, and mindfulness-based approaches.

Frequently Asked Questions



What kinds of difficulties can we help with?

Some examples include:

  • depression and low mood/motivation
  • anxiety
  • attention-deficit disorder
  • stress and burnout at work, school or in your relationships
  • perfectionism

How does it work?

Appointments are via a secure telehealth platform that is easy and convenient to use. Frequency of sessions can vary and the optimum frequency is determined based on your specific needs in consultation with your psychologist. Usually, treatment is time-limited, and involves meeting at regular intervals to support personal change and monitor progress.

When you contact us at Clarity Psychological Services, we will reach out within 48 hours to discuss how we might help address specific problems or improve your quality of life.
