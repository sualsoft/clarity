Coaching services involve specific goal setting in the service of personal growth. We can work with you to develop a customized plan of action to help you achieve personal goals, including:

  • finding a new career that suits your skills and talents
  • building community and improving your current relationships
  • improving your personal productivity and your overall health and wellbeing
  • creating a new habit or changing an old habit
  • dating support and relationship coaching
